ABOUT     PHASS     NET
HISTORY *

PHASS NET was founded in 1998 by entrepreneur Anya Sharma,
initially operating out of a single garage with just two vans.
Anya, a former logistics manager, identified a gap in the market for reliable,
same-day deliveries within her city.
Through word-of-mouth and a relentless focus on customer satisfaction,
the company steadily grew. By 2005,
PHASS NET had expanded its operations to cover an entire region, and by 2015,
it had established a nationwide network,
leveraging advanced tracking technology and a diverse fleet.
A significant milestone was reached in 2020 with the launch of its eco-friendly delivery initiative,
incorporating electric vehicles and sustainable packaging.

MISSIONS *

PHASS NET's mission is to empower businesses and individuals by providing swift,
secure, and sustainable delivery solutions that connect communities and drive commerce.
We are committed to leveraging innovation and a customer-centric approach
to ensure every parcel reaches its destination efficiently,
reliably, and with minimal environmental impact.
Our core values include speed, integrity, transparency,
and a dedication to continuous improvement in all aspects of our service.
